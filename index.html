Key Projects

THERMIFLECT - HEAT REFLECTIVE COATING

ThermiFlect is an advanced heat-reflective coating designed to enhance energy efficiency and sustainability. By significantly reducing solar heat absorption, ThermiFlect keeps surfaces cooler, leading to lower energy costs and improved comfort. Ideal for roofs, cement slabs, and various outdoor surfaces, this innovative solution supports a greener, more sustainable future.

MEGA FIELD - NON CHEMICAL FOOD PRESERVATION

Mega Field is our cutting-edge technology designed to revolutionize food preservation. This innovative solution significantly enhances the shelf life and quality of food products without compromising taste or nutritional value. Engineered for efficiency and reliability, Mega Field represents the future of food preservation, ensuring freshness and safety with minimal energy consumption.

HYDROGEN ENERGY

Our latest research project focuses on the innovative production and utilization of hydrogen as a clean fuel for 4-stroke engines. We are pioneering new methods to produce and store hydrogen safely and cost-effectively. By integrating hydrogen into traditional 4-stroke engines, we aim to enhance efficiency, reduce emissions, and pave the way for sustainable energy solutions. This project opens new avenues for eco-friendly transportation and energy systems, contributing to a greener future.
